Skill Classes

In these classes you learn to cook one subject several different ways

These classes deep-dive into a single subject and take 3 hours to complete. Listed below are the types of one-on-one classes we offer. The price varies based on number of people. Price is: $125 for 1 person, $240 for 2 people, $360 for 3 people, $450 for 4 people and $500 for 5 people. Once you complete your creation, you will sit down and enjoy the meal.

Knife Skills Class

Learn about the various types of knives used in the kitchen, while improving your skills on the key cutting techniques. In this class you will learn to chop, dice, mince, julienne, chiffonade, and slice in order to understand why the knife tang is important. You will create a delicious vegetable soup with a cheesy ciabatta toast.
