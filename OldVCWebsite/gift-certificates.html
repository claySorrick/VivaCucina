Gift Certificates

Cooking classes make a great gift that keeps on giving. We offer gift certificates for our One-On-One classes where you pick the date, time and recipe or desired skill to learn. These are good for the beginner or experienced cook and we can accommodate up to five people in this type of class.

Call us today to purchase your gift certificate over the phone. We take check, credit and debit card. We will email you the beautiful designed certificate and the recipe/lesson document to include with your gift. You can print the certificate on your printer and we will keep track of it in our files, making it an easy and quick way for you to give a delicious and useful gift. Click on the "One-On-One Classes" tab for more information about these classes.
