Private Group Classes & Menu

What a great way to spend time with family, friends, coworkers, and clients
It’s like having your own private party without all the work, in Viva Cucina’s large beautiful kitchen. You choose the menu, invite your guests and spend time cooking, eating and laughing together. Viva Cucina conducts private group classes for six students, to as many as twenty-six students in a class. Your group will be divided into teams and guided through hands-on recipe preparation, then dine together. Guests receive a package of the class recipes to take home. The class takes about 3 hours to conduct from start to finish. Viva Cucina takes care of the details; menu planning, shopping, ingredients, instruction, kitchen setup, cleanup, and aprons to wear while cooking.
